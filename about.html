Our story

Your Trusted Towing Partner in Dallas

To provide fast, fair, and professional towing and roadside assistance to every customer, every time.

Hala Texas Towing was founded with a simple mission: help stranded drivers get back on the road quickly and affordably. As a locally owned business, we understand the stress of a breakdown and are committed to being the reliable solution our neighbors deserve.

Over the years, we've grown from a single truck to a fleet serving all of Dallas. Our team shares a passion for helping others and a dedication to transparency and quality. We're proud to be a trusted name in Dallas towing.
