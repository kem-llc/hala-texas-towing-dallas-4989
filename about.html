Our story

About Hala Texas Towing

Our mission is to provide fast, fair, and professional towing and roadside assistance to the Dallas community, 24/7.

Hala Texas Towing was founded with a simple goal: be the towing company Dallas can rely on. Our founder, a lifelong Dallas resident, saw a need for honest, transparent service in an industry often plagued by hidden fees and slow response times. Starting with a single truck, we built a reputation for showing up fast and treating every customer like a neighbor.

Today, we operate a modern fleet and serve thousands of customers each year. We're proud to be locally owned and deeply committed to our city. Every call we answer is a chance to make someone's bad day a little better — and we take that responsibility seriously.
