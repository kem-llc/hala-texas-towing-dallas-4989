Flatbed Towing

Safe, damage-free transport for all vehicles.

Schedule this service

Overview

Flatbed towing is the safest way to transport vehicles, especially those with all-wheel drive or low ground clearance. Our flatbed trucks ensure your vehicle is fully secured and off the ground.

What's included

  • No wear on tires or drivetrain
  • Ideal for luxury, classic, or damaged vehicles
  • Easy loading and unloading

How it works

1

Position

We position the flatbed truck and prepare the ramp.

2

Load

Your vehicle is carefully driven or winched onto the flatbed.

3

Secure & Deliver

Straps secure the vehicle, and we transport it safely.

Common questions

Is flatbed towing more expensive?

It can be slightly more, but the safety benefits often outweigh the cost. We offer competitive rates.

Do you tow AWD vehicles?

Yes, flatbed towing is recommended for AWD to prevent drivetrain damage.
